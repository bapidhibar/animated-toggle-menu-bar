About

WELCOME TO RIMMIK INFRASTRUCTURE PRIVATE LIMITED


We are committed to a high standard of excellence in all activities that strive to establish a strong relationship of mutual trust and confidence with clients. On any given project, our team is committed to provide services at all times to the client's satisfaction. Converted to limited company in 2000 and with high standard and skill and determination ARSS has grown to become one of the leading construction companies in Orissa and one among the largest multi-disciplined construction companies in Eastern India. Our aim is to provide high quality construction services in a smooth, convenient and impressive manner which will automatically influence our clients to recommend services of our company to others.
